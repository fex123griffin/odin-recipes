Classic Hamburger

Can you handle this burger?

A traditional hamburger made on most American grills.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Buns

Steps

  1. Prehead grill on the max heat level
  2. Season hamburger patties and let them get to room temp
  3. Grill until burgers are 165 degrees
  4. Dress burger to your liking
