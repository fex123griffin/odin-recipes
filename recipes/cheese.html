American Philly Cheese-Steak

An American Classic

A Philadelphia favorite for those who seek flavor.

Ingredients

  • Shaved Beef
  • Mushrooms
  • Peppers
  • Carmalized onions
  • 1 slice of mozarella cheese
  • Long Buns

Steps

  1. Prehead griddle on the max heat level
  2. Cook shaved beef with peppers, onions, and mushrooms
  3. Cook until meat is browned
  4. Put mozarella on the shaved beef with extras
  5. Serve on a long bun
